What is Derivita

Derivita is a STEM technology platform and powerful computer algebra system that supports deeper and more complex question types. Derivita offers an elegant user interface that vastly improves the student and educator experience. Derivita can be used with any publisher's textbook or open education resource (OER) and seamlessly integrates with any learning management system (LMS).

Our Mission

The Derivita team is built of math enthusiasts, former educators, technologists, and EdTech gurus! We believe that teaching and learning math should be both a fun and convenient experience and that technology should help solve the daily challenges educators face.

With this belief, we strive to ensure that the Derivita toolbox is meeting the most critical needs and wants of our educators. Our mission is simple: create a successful mathematical in-person or online learning experience for all.

Culture and Values

At Derivita, our core values help drive all that we do, the technology and products that we build, and the services we provide.

  1. We embrace the power of inclusion and diversity of thought, opinion, belief, education, experience, religion, race, gender, age, and sexual orientation.
  2. We condemn racial prejudice in any form.
  3. We believe that Mathematics and STEM education is a gateway to college and career success and that we can play a role in providing greater opportunity to students, parents, and teachers.
  4. We believe in equality and equity of opportunity and access for all learners.
  5. We value every team member's contributions.
  6. We treat others with respect. Everyone is valued as an individual.

POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

PAY PERIOD AND CHECK DISTRIBUTION

Employees will be paid on a semi-monthly basis. Regular paydays occur on the 5th and 20th of each month. If paydays fall on non-workdays or holidays, employees will be paid on the Friday before the regularly scheduled payday.

DIRECT DEPOSIT POLICY

It is the policy of Derivita to issue employee payments solely through electronic direct deposit. Direct deposit provides many benefits for employees, including greater security and faster access to funds. Checks will not be issued.

Employee payments will be electronically deposited directly into one or more checking or savings accounts designated by each employee. Accounts must be established with financial institutions, such as banks or credit unions that support direct deposit.
